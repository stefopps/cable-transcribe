MEETING OVERVIEW
This session focused on preparing staff to handle patient care and discharge processes, with a emphasis on thorough assessments of patients' needs.

HIPAA/PHI
GROUP: HIPAA Compliance
HIGHLIGHT: Ensure all personal health information (PHI) is handled securely and in accordance with regulations.
- Review PHI handling procedures and ensure staff understand confidentiality and data security protocols

BENEFITS
GROUP: Benefits for Patients and Staff
HIGHLIGHT: Utilize benefits to support patients' needs, including assistance programs and housing services.
- Identify eligible patients for assistance programs and provide resources for access
- Develop plan to communicate benefits information to patients

IT SYSTEMS
GROUP: IT Systems Updates
HIGHLIGHT: Ensure all necessary systems are updated before discharge to ensure patient safety and data integrity.
- Update Symplr/NetLearning system to reflect recent changes
- Verify login credentials for Day 3 meeting

ONBOARDING LOGISTICS
GROUP: Onboarding Logistics
HIGHLIGHT: Coordinate onboarding process with social workers to support patient needs post-discharge.
- Schedule social worker meetings for patients before discharge
- Develop plan for follow-up care and resources after discharge

ACTION ITEMS & NEXT STEPS
* Send reminders to staff about patient care and discharge processes
* Follow up with HR department regarding benefits communication
* Verify IT system updates are complete by Day 3 meeting
* Coordinate with social workers for pre-discharge meetings

UNANSWERED QUESTIONS
None noted.

MOST_IMPORTANT: Ensure thorough assessments of patients' needs to provide effective support during and after discharge.
