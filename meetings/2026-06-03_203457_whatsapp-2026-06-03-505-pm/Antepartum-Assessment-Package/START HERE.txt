ANTEPARTUM ASSESSMENT — YOUR CLASS PACKAGE
==========================================

Everything is in this one folder. Copy the whole folder to your PC
(or unzip the ZIP file Steve sent you). You do not need to install anything.


WHAT IS IN THIS FOLDER
----------------------

  Class-Recording.mp4
      The original class audio/video (~34 minutes).

  Antepartum Assessment - Study Guide.docx
      Short summary and study notes by body system. START WITH THIS.

  Antepartum Assessment - Full Transcript.docx
      Everything that was said, with timestamps. Use Find (Ctrl+F) to search.


EASIEST WAY TO OPEN THINGS
--------------------------

  Double-click one of these:

    1 - PLAY CLASS RECORDING.bat     → plays the recording
    2 - OPEN STUDY GUIDE.bat         → opens the study guide in Word
    3 - OPEN FULL TRANSCRIPT.bat     → opens the full transcript in Word

  Or double-click the .mp4 or .docx files directly.


IF WORD ASKS YOU TO ENABLE EDITING
----------------------------------

  Click "Enable Editing" or just read the document — either is fine.


IF THE RECORDING WILL NOT PLAY
------------------------------

  Right-click Class-Recording.mp4 → Open with → choose Films & TV,
  Windows Media Player, or VLC if you have it.


ABOUT THE TRANSCRIPT
--------------------

  The written transcript was made by computer from the recording.
  Some words may be wrong. Use your instructor and your course books
  for anything clinical at the bedside.
