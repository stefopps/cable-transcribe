**MEETING OVERVIEW**

This session was an introductory orientation for new employees at RWJ Barnabas Health, covering various topics related to onboarding, training, and benefits enrollment.

**TOPIC GROUPS**

### Benefits
HIGHLIGHT: Employees must enroll in benefits within 30 days or during open enrollment.
- Benefits are life-altering events can be used to enroll outside of this period.
- Open enrollment typically starts in October or November, but benefits won't take effect until January 1st.

### High Reliability Organization (HRO) Training
HIGHLIGHT: The training emphasizes prioritizing patient safety and well-being, with a focus on speaking up and following procedures.
- Safety is a key component of HRO training, with a SAFETY acronym: S - Speak up for Safety; A - Acknowledge another's pool; F - Follow procedure; E - Empathy with the employee; T - Teamwork; Y - You/your own behavior.

### IT Systems and Onboarding Logistics
HIGHLIGHT: Employees will have access to a service portal for questions and concerns, with login instructions provided.
- The service portal is called Symplr and can be accessed from any device with a browser.
- A separate email with next steps and training details will be sent after Day 2.
